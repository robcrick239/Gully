Sugar Apple

Scientific Name

The scientific name for Sugar Apple is “Annona squamosa”.

Origin

Sugar Apple is native to tropical South America and the West Indies.

Health Benefits

Sugar Apple has many health benefits. Some of them are:

Sugar Apples lower high blood pressure and high cholesterol

Sugar Apples prevent heart problems

Sugar apples are extremely high in vitamin C and are a good source of vitamin B6 and fibre, and they contain many other vitamins and minerals.

Fun Facts!

Some fun facts about sugar apples are:

Around 20 to 40 black seeds can be found in most sugar apple varieties, which contain a toxin and thus should not be consumed.

Sugar Apples are also known as ‘ata’, ‘aati’, ‘annon’, ‘sweetsop’, ‘pineapple sugar-apple’.
