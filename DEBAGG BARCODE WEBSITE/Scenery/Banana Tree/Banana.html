Banana Tree

Scientific Name

The scientific name for the banana tree is "Musa acuminata"

Origin

Banana trees are said to have been early cultivated in the South East Asia.

Banana trees were then taken from the Canary Islands and brought to the Caribbean by the Europeans.

Health Benefits

Bananas help with the regulation of blood pressure.

The effectiveness of potassium found in bananas has been scientifically proven to counteract high blood pressure and heart disease

Bananas are also soothing to the gastrointestinal tract

Fun Facts!

Some fun facts about bananas are:

Bananas float in water because they are less dense in comparison.

The banana is actually classified as a berry.

The genetic similarity between a human and a banana is 60%.
