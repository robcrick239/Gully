Turmeric

Scientific Name

The scientific name for Turmeric is "Curcuma longa"

Origin

Turmeric is native to southern India and Indonesia.

It is widely cultivated on the mainland and in the islands of the Indian Ocean.

Health Benefits

Taking turmeric by mouth seems to reduce hay fever symptoms such as sneezing, itching, runny nose, and congestion.

The spice is reputed to relieve arthritis pain.

Most research shows that taking curcumin, a chemical found in turmeric, by mouth reduces depression symptoms in people already using an antidepressant.

Fun Facts!

Some fun facts about Turmeric are:

In ancient times it was used as a perfume as well as a spice.

Turmeric paste is a home remedy for sunburn and it is also an ingredient in many commercial sunscreens.

Turmeric is the ingredient in curry that gives it a vibrant yellow color.
