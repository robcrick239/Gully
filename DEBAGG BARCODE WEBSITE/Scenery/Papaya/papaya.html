Papaya

Scientific Name

The scientific name for the papaya is "Carica papaya "

Origin

Different authors suggest a Mesoamerican origin of C. papaya, comprising southern Mexico to Central America (Vavilov, 1926; Storey, 1976), although no direct archaeological evidence regarding the center of origin of papaya has been reported as yet (Fuentes and Santamaría, 2014).

Health Benefits

Papaya has many health benefits and some are:

•Papaya is rich in valuable nutrients

•Papaya also possess powerful antioxidants

•It may also defend against the visible signs of aging, helping your skin remain smooth and youthful.
