Palm Tree

Scientific Name

The scientific name for the Palm Tree is "Arecaceae"

Origin

The palm tree has been traced to have origins in India, Northern Africa, regions of Southeast Asia, and the South Pacific Islands.

Depending on the species of palm tree, the origin of the palm tree would vary.

Health Benefits

Cabbage palm berries and seeds were used to treat headaches and fevers by native peoples of the southeastern United States.

Juice from small, crushed roots of dwarf palmetto can be used as a counter-irritant for sore eyes.

High blood pressure and kidney problems can be treated from the dried roots.

Fun Facts!

There are 3 palm trees native to our island Barbados and they are:

The Cabbage palm (Roystonea oleracea)

The Macaw palm (Aiphanes minima)

The Thatch palm (Coccothrinax barbadensis)
