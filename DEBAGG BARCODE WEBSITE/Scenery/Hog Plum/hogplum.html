Hog Plum

Scientific Name

The hog plum goes by the scientific name “Spondias purpurea”.

Origin

The hog plum was early cultivated from the area of Southern Mexico to northern Peru.

They were later found to be capable of growing in the West Indies also.

Health Benefits

The hog plum has many health benefits and some are:

• The ability to strengthen the immune system

• They may help with weight loss

• They have the ability to give you more energy

Fun Facts!

Some fun facts about hog plum are:

The hog plum is also called the jocote or Spanish plum.

The fruit could be used to remove stains from clothing.
