Scientific Name

The scientific name for suriname cherry is "Eugenia Uniflora "

Origin

Surinam Cherry is native to South America; it is found from Guyana to southern Brazil and northern Uruguay. It was introduced to Bermuda as a garden tree because it produces an edible fruit.
