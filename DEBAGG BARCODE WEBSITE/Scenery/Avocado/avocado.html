Avocado

Scientific Name

The avocado, also called the alligator pear goes under the scientific name “Persea Americana”.

Origin

The avocado tree is native to Mexico in the Western Hemisphere. The avocado got to the Caribbean via the Spaniards and it has the scientific name “P. americana variety americana”.

The tree is grown in abundance in some parts of South and North America, as well as Asia and some parts of Oceania.

Health Benefits

Avocados have a plethora of health benefits such as:

• They are nutrient rich

• They help to improve vision

• They are healthy for the heart

• They also help to improve digestion

Fun Facts!

Some fun facts about avocado are:

The popular dish guacamole is a derivation of the Aztec word “ahuacamolli”, which translates loosely to “avocado soup” or “avocado sauce.”
