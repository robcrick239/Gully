Mother-in-Law Tongue

Scientific Name

The commonly known "Mother-in-Law" plant or "Snake Plant" goes by the scientific name "Sansevieria trifasciata"

Origin

This plant has originated in West Africa, namely Nigeria and China also. This plant also arrived to the Cariibean through the Europeans.

Health Benefits

This plant helps with the purification of breathing air. The plant also possesses anti-septic properties which is great for skin care!

Fun Facts!

In China, this plant is said to be a good luck charm. Also in West Africa, this plant was used to ward off evil spirits.

Here in Barbados, this plant is called a "money plant."
