Sandbox

Scientific Name

The scientific name for the Sandbox plant is "Hura crepitans".

Origin

The Sandbox tree is native primarily to tropical parts of South America and the Amazonian Rainforest. It is sometimes found in the tropical parts of North America as well.

Health Benefits

Some of the many health benefits of the sandbox tree are:

• Sandbox tree is used to treat skin diseases, rheumatism, and intestinal worms.

• Sandbox tree leaves are used to treat eczema.

Fun Facts!

Some fun facts about the Sandbox tree are:

The tree gets its name from little bowls made from the fruit that was once used to hold fine, dry sand.

The tree is nicknamed as 'Monkey-no-climb native to tropical regions of North and South America
