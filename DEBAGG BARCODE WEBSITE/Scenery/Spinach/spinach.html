Spinach

Scientific Name

The scientific name for Spinach is “Spinacia oleracea”

Origin

Spinach originally came from ancient Persia (now Iran and surrounding countries) where it was known as aspanakh

Health Benefits

Spinach has many health benefits and some are:

•Spinach is beneficial to eye health

•Spinach aids in wound recovery

•Spinach also strengthens the immune system

Fun Facts!

Some fun facts about spinach are:

Medieval artists extracted green pigment from spinach to use as ink or paint.

The creator of the comic strip character, Popeye, E.C. Segar was a vegetarian and as a way of promoting the benefits of vegetables, he boosted his character’s strength with a known iron-rich food. Popeye has been portrayed since 1931. As a result of this show, U.S. spinach growers credited Popeye with a 33% increase in domestic spinach consumption – a welcome boost to an industry during the depression era.
