Aloe Vera

Scientific Name

Ironically, Aloe vera has no scientific name. It is just Aloe vera!

Origin

There was many a debate as to the origin of Aloe vera.

Research suggests that Aloe vera originated in the Arabian peninsula.

Health Benefits

Aloe vera has a number of health benefits with some being:

• The gel from aloe vera can be used as an ointment to treat skin and gums

• Aloe vera can also be used as a remedy for diabetic symptoms.

Fun Facts!

Some fun facts about Aloe vera are:

The botanical name of Aloe vera is Aloe barbadensis miller.

Egyptian Queen Cleopatra was believed to use Aloe vera gel as part of her skin beauty routine.
