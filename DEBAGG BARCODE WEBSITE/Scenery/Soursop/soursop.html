Soursop

Scientific Name

The scientific name for soursop is "Annona muricata L. "

Origin

Soursop is a tropical fruit, native to South American countries (Mexico, Brazil, Cuba, Peru, and Venezuela). However, this plant is widely cultivated in South East Asia, Pacific regions, and North Africa.

Health Benefits

Soursop has many health benefits and some are:

•Soursop is high in antioxidants

•Soursop also helps to stave off inflammation

•Soursop also stabilises blood sugar levels

Fun Facts!

Some fun facts about soursop are:

The fruit is usually called soursop due to its slightly acidic taste when ripe.
