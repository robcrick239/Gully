Mango

Scientific Name

The mango goes by the scientific name “Mangifera indica”.

Origin

The mango is indigenous to southern Asia, especially the Assam and Myanmar states of India.

However, the mango did not make its way to the Caribbean until the 1700s.

Health Benefits

The mango has a number of health benefits with some being:

• The mango is nutrient rich

• The mango also helps diabetes patients with its ability to lower the risk factors associated with the disease.

• Mangos also help with hair and skin quality due to the level vitamin A that it possesses.

Fun Facts!

Some fun facts about mango are:

The mango is related to cashews and pistachios

Mango trees can live for centuries, with the oldest tree thought to be around 300 years old.
