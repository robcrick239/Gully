Golden Apple

Scientific Name

The scientific name for Golden Apple is “ Spondias dulcis”.

Origin

Native to Polynesia, it was introduced into Jamaica in 1782 and again ten years later by Captain Bligh, and can now be found growing throughout the Caribbean, Asia, South and Central American regions and, to a lesser extent, in Africa.

Health Benefits

Golden Apple has many health benefits. Some of them are:

It helps in the renewal of tissues and protects against cardiovascular diseases.

In some places, the fruit is used to treat injuries from burns.

Scientific studies have also proven that the fruit can lower high blood pressure.

The leaves of the tree are dried and ground until powdery and used to treat mouth sores.

Fun Facts!

Some fun facts about golden apples are:

The best way to eat golden apples is raw with a little salt.
