Granadilla

Scientific Name

The granadilla goes by the scientific name “Passiflora ligularis”

Origin

The granadilla is native to Andes of South America and Central America.

Health Benefits

The granadilla has many health benefits such as:

• Being good for the heart

• Boosting the immune system

• Being rich in antioxidants

Fun Facts!

Some fun facts about the granadilla are:

The granadilla and the passion fruit are different, but they are indeed related.

They are berries.

Granadillas are sweeter than passion fruits.
