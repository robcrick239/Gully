Lime

Scientific Name

The scientific name for the Lime is “Citrus aurantifolia”.

Origin

Lime is believed to be native to northern India and adjoining parts of Myanmar, or in northern Malaysia.

Health Benefits

Some health benefits that lime is used for are:

• Treatment for Arthritis, Cholera, Gout, Piles, Scurvy, the fever and the common cold.

• It is also beneficial to the heart.

Fun Facts!

Some fun facts about the Lime Trees are:

Lime is called the powerhouse of flavours.

One lime tree can produce over 1000 fruits annually
