Plantain

Scientific Name

The scientific name for the plantain is the "Plantago"

Origin

Plantains are believed to have originated in Southeast Asia.

Health Benefits

The mango has a number of health benefits with some being:

• Plantains are high in iron, which is essential for the production of red blood cells.

• Plantains may reduce cardiovascular risk and improve mood.

• Plantains may also help support the immune system.

Fun Facts!

Some fun facts about plantain are:

Many modern plantains are hybrids derived from the cross of two wild species, the Musa acuminata and Musa balbisiana.

The fruit bears all year long!
