Bayleaf

Scientific Name

The bay leaf, also called the laurel leaf goes under the name “Laurus nobilis”.

Origin

The bay leaf is native to the Mediterranean but is now widely grown in Central and North America as well as in some parts of Asia and Europe.

The bay leaf native to the West Indies is called “Pimenta Racemosa”

Health Benefits

Bay leaf is shown to possess antibacterial properties. These antibacterial properties can prohibit the growth of E.coli and other bacteria which can cause ulcers and even cancer.

Bay leaf also decrease blood sugar levels in persons with type 2 diabetes.

Fun Facts!

Some fun facts about bayleaf are:

Bay leaf is a very popular seasoning plant used in a variety of dishes.

Ancient Greeks saw the bay leaf as a symbol of honour.
