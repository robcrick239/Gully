Passion Fruit

Scientific Name

The scientific name for passion fruit is "Passiflora edulis".

Origin

Passion Fruit is native to Southern Brazil through Paraguay and Northern Argentina.

Health Benefits

There are numerous health benefits associated with passion fruit. Some of these being:

• Passion Fruit is full of antioxidants such as Vitamin C and, Vitamin A. These antioxidants help your body heal, lower inflammation, and protect your cells from damage.

• Passion fruit is also a source of fibre that keeps your bowels healthy and moving.

• Passion Fruit helps to lower cholesterol.

• Passion Fruit boosts immune system.

Fun Facts!

Some fun facts about passion fruit are:

Passion fruit comes from a family of passion flowers(Passiflora).

In Indonesia, there are two types of passion fruit, white flesh and yellow flesh.

The passion fruit is known as lilikoi in Hawaii.
