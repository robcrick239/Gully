Guava Tree

Scientific Name

The scientific name for Guava is “Psidium guajava”.

Origin

Guava is native to Central and South America.

Health Benefits

Guava has several health benefits. Some of them being:

Guava is used to help build immunity.

Guava improves eyesight.

Guava improves skin health.

Fun Facts!

Some fun facts about Guava are:

In the Philippines, ripe guavas are used to flavour a soup called “sinigang”.

Guavas are sometimes referred to as “super-fruits” because of their high vitamin content.

There are around 150 species of guava that can be found in tropical and sub-tropical areas around the world.
